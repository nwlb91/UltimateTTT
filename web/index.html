How to play

The big board is a 3×3 grid of nine small Tic-Tac-Toe boards.

  1. Your move decides your opponent's board. If you play in the top-right square of a small board, your opponent must next play in the top-right small board.
  2. Win a small board (three in a row) to claim it on the big board.
  3. Win three small boards in a row to win the game.
  4. Free move: if you're sent to a board that's already won or full, you may play in any open board.

Highlighted squares are your legal moves. The glowing board is where you must play.
